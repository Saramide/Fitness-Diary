Omu Resort is located at a serene environment. We strive to create a unique experience by taking you away from the hustle and bustle of the city of Lagos. Upon your arrival you are welcome in by our butler who will greet you with your room key and take your bags. From there you may choose to relax immediately in the Lounge while our attentive staff fixe your drinks or you can choose to retire immediately with the option of private dining directly in your room. Guests also enjoy strolling through our vast gardens, or unwindling with some high life music. No matter how you choose to spend your time at the Omu Resort, rest assured that you will enjoy the timeless luxury of a place untouched by demands of the world.
